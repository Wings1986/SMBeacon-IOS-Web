Tiny Dumplings

at WP24 in Los Angeles, CA

Pork Belly, Black Vinegar, Chili Oil, Ginger, Cilantro Leaves. The other Dumplings we had could not come out from behind the shadow of these delicious “Tiny Dumplings”. I loved how a Cilantro Leaf accompanied each Dumpling; such care was taken to provide the perfect balance of flavor.
